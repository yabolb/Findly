
# Article Guidelines for Radar

## Capitalization Rules (Spanish Orthography)
Follow strict Spanish orthographic rules for titles and headers:

1.  **Sentence Case**: Only capitalize the **first word** of the sentence and **proper nouns**.
    *   ❌ Incorrect: "Los Mejores Regalos Para San Valentín"
    *   ✅ Correct: "Los mejores regalos para San Valentín"
    
2.  **Proper Nouns**: Always capitalize names of brands, holidays, people, and specific product models.
    *   Examples: "San Valentín", "Nike", "Sony", "iPhone", "Navidad".

3.  **After Colon**: Use **lowercase** for the first word after a colon, unless it is a proper noun or a quote.
    *   ❌ Incorrect: "1. Toque de luz: Embellecedor de labios..."
    *   ✅ Correct: "1. Toque de luz: embellecedor de labios..."

## Structure
-   Use `<h2>` for main sections.
-   Use `[PRODUCT:N]` placeholders for product embeds.

## Future Articles
When creating new drafts or articles, ALWAYS apply these rules before generating the JSON.
